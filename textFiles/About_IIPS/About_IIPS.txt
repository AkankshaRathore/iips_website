<p style="text-align:justify;">

International Institute of Professional Studies, a department of Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya was set up  in 1992. With the quality in the content, scope and professionalism of its programs, IIPS has emerged as one of the best centers of technical education in central India and is recognized by AICTE. It has been placed in the 'A' category and ranked 24th overall in the Business Standard Survey of best BE Schools of India.

</p>

<p style="text-align:justify;">
This martinet institute provides ample opportunities to students for enhancing the knowledge according to their professional and personal interests and goals.IIPS  offers integrated post-graduate courses in the fields of computer and management. In addition,institute serves specialized courses in advertisement,tourism and public relations management as well. Our students are placed in the best companies of the world.
</p>
